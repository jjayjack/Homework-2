About Me

Hello there! My name is Jocelyn and this is my site that showcases projects and activities that I have done while coding. I am excited to start learning new languages and diving deep within the code. I am originally from Northwest Indiana where I spent time with my loving parents and sister. After high school, I went to Purdue University where I studied Nutrition, Fitness and Health and graduated with my Bachelor Degree. Once I graduated, I was undecided what I wanted to do with my degree. I decided to take some time off from school and took a position with Lowe's to help me work on paying off my student loans. Once I finished paying off my loan I decided that it was time to figure out what I wanted to do next. I had moved to Chicago with my boyfriend and had the time to think about what I wanted to do. This led me to evaluate what I enjoy doing and pointed me toward pursuing Trilogy's Bootcamp through Northwestern.
